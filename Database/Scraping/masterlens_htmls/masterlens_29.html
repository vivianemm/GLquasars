DescriptionThe Bullet Cluster is one of the hottest, most X-ray luminous clusters known, with numerous multiply-imaged background sources, and a giant arc on the NW side of the main CD (with measured background source redshift). Multicolor HST-ACS (BVI-bands), Magellan (BVR-bands) and VLT (I-band) imaging is available.
